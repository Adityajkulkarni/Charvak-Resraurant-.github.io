ABOUT US

Charvaka restaurant is a family run business promoted by two individuals.The Firm was established in the year 2014 at Bangalore in the state of Karnataka with a vision to cater the demands/needs of HORECA segment with a wide range of product options. The products offered by Guru Krupa foods products are available in attractive, hygienic and classy packaging. We have been providing our clients with the best by offering healthy and safe to consume products having rich taste, purity and natural aroma, We guarantee a traditional natural taste in healthy form, to achieve this we use IQF & Blast Freezers this process freezes the food in such a way that each individual piece of food is frozen separately from all others, during this process No Chemical additives or preservatives are added the products are free from any type of contamination and impurities thus the products are as healthy as natural as homemade (authentic taste) due to which their demand is increasing day by day.
Guru Krupa foods Products are processed at a HACCP certified processing units, Guru Krupa Foods have been offering complete satisfaction to the clients when it comes to quality and freshness of the products, GKF has became a trusted name in the market of Frozen food Products due to the following factors:


Wide range of Products (Veg, Non-Veg, Sea Food, Can Foods and Dairy Products).

  • Taste
  • Uniform Pricing
  • Long Shelf Life
  • No Wastage
  • No Labour Requirement

  • Economical Pricing of all the Products.
